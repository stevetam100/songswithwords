STORY

Steve Tam plays records the way a good host builds an evening — patiently, generously, and in service of the dance floor.

Based in New York, he plays everywhere from private events, to hotels and brand moments, to dark warehouses and sunrises over the playa — rooms where the music is part of the architecture, rather than the backdrop.
