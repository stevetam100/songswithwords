TREATED
Treatment recipe: saturate .8 · brightness .82 · contrast 1.06 · sepia .14, then film grain at 22% overlay. Photos sit behind black gradient scrims when text is on top. Blends and extensions must be seamless — no visible seams, matched grain and color. Captions in 9px tracked caps, bottom-left.
06 — LOOK & FEEL
DARK & CINEMATIC
One full-bleed frame per page. Near-black grounds, red light roaming softly behind everything, grain over everything at 6%.
SLOW & SUBTLE
Everything fades — nothing jumps. Backgrounds settle over 2.4–3.2s; text follows ~.5s later. Pure opacity, gentle ease-in-out, long loops.
HAIRLINE STRUCTURE
1px sand rules at 14% divide space; verticals meet horizontals exactly. Generous negative space; density is the exception.
QUIET LUXURY
Understated over loud. One ember accent per view, tracked caps, no ornament. The room is premium; the voice is calm.
